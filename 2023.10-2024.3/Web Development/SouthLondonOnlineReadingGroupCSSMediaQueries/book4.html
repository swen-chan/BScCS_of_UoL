Robinson Crusoe by Daniel Defoe

Robinson Crusoe is a novel by Daniel Defoe, first published on 25 April 1719, which sees the main character who lived all alone for 28 years in an un-inhabited Island on the Coast of America.

Current Discussion: What feelings did this book evoke for you?

A particular question from the book chapter

Eddie Hendrix

I last read this at about 10 years of age and I am now over 50. It cannot be that the story has improved over the intervening 40 years so it must just be that I didn't pay attention then but I have now.
26 October 2018

Alessandra Lim

Absolutely gripping. A redemption story, like all the best ones. As an instinctive problem-solver I was fascinated by his resourcefulness in the face of necessity.
02 September 2018
