Welcome to the Online Reading Group page. Here you can find a list of books being read by online members and the protocol you should follow for joining any one of the groups and read along with the other members.

In order to obtain the best experience while reading and reviewing books, please follow our Online Reading Protocol.

  1. Scroll the page to check all the book titles which are currently being read.
  2. You can view a book by either clicking on the book link or its logo.
  3. You can read, comment or check current discussions about the book you have selected.

Here are the books currently being read by online members:

The Design of Everyday Things by Don Norman: read

The Design of Everyday Things is a best-selling book by cognitive scientist and usability engineer Donald Norman about how design serves as the communication between object and user.

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone by J. K. Rowling: read

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone is the 1st fantasy novel written by British author J. K. Rowling which reveals a parallel magic world.

And Then There Were None by Agatha Christie: read

And Then There Were None is a mystery novel by English writer Agatha Christie, her best selling novel and described by her as the most difficult of her books to write.

Robinson Crusoe by Daniel Defoe: read

Robinson Crusoe is a novel by Daniel Defoe, first published on 25 April 1719, which sees the main character who lived all alone for 28 years in an un-inhabited Island on the Coast of America.

Alice's Adventures in Wonderland by Lewis Carroll: read

Alice's Adventures in Wonderland is an 1865 novel written by English author Lewis Carroll which tells of a girl named Alice falling through a rabbit hole into a fantasy world populated by peculiar, anthropomorphic creatures.
