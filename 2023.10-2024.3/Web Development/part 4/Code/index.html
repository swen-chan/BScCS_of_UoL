Marvel Universe Theme Park





About Us

Welcome to the Marvel Universe Theme Park located in the heart of Bavaria. We offer you the opportunity to step into the world of your favorite superheroes and supervillains, experiencing the magic and excitement of the Marvel Universe. Alongside our incredible attractions, we extend the chance for visitors to indulge in a hotel stay, savor the delightful atmosphere and cuisine at our restaurants, and witness exceptional shows featuring your favorite Marvel characters.

Our working hours are: Monday to Sunday 10.00 - 23.00 CET

RIDES

From small to big, Marvel Universe Theme Park invites all to partake in the thrilling escapades each of our rides offers. Select from our spectacular array of rides, including : Into The Spiderverse, The Superhero Slide, Hulk Smash, Dr. Strange's Mystical Realm, Guardians of the Galaxy Cosmos, The Avengers Tower, Wakanda Waters, Avengers Splash Zone, Ant-Man Microverse, Thor's Hammer Swing and Wolverine's X-Treme Adventure, and embark on an unforgettable adventure.
SHOWS

Do you need a break from the rides? We offer you the opportunity to further discover the amazing Marvel Universe. Enjoy the shows with your favorite superhero on every Tuesday and/or Saturday. Pick a date, dress like a superhero, and perhaps you will become our new Superstar.
HOTELS

If you would like to extend the duration of your experience at our park, we offer you the opportunity to discover the haven of comfort located in the heart of our park. Our park caters to the needs of visitors of every age and therefore the rooms are equipped accordingly. Whether you are here with your friends or with your family, our hotel is your gateway to a memorable stay in the Marvel Universe.
RESTAURANTS

Marvel Universe Restaurant is the place where culinary excellence meets warm ambience and hospitality. Whether you are visiting us for a short time or are planning a memorable event, our restaurant will impress you with the variety of the dishes served. Our menu is prepared with passion and creativity and will take you on a marvelous gastronomic journey. Join us for the multitude of flavors, where every dish tells a story.
