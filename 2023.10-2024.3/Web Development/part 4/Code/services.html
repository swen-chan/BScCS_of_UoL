Transport Services

At the theme park we have a wide selection of transport services. The whole theme park is wheelchair accessible and we have golf carts, trams, push buggies and much more available on site so we can suit your every need.
We have 5 on site medical centers available, all with step free access. If in the case of an emergency our medical team is not fully equipped for, we have a medical team helicopter available so you can be transported to the nearest major hospital as soon as possible.

Theme Park Map

Map Key:

: Park Entrance

The entrance and exit of the park is located where the green arrow is on the map! If you need assistance there are many staff available here.

: Rides & Attractions

Each ride or attractions is located on the Park Map by a red circle. If you would like to see our full list of rides and what they offer please click here.

: Toilets

Throughout our park we have many restroom facilities available, the services are free to use and also have accessibility for wheelchair users as well as diaper changing facilities.

: Information Center & Wheelchair Services

Near the entrance of our park we have our Information Center where our Wheelchair support team is located. Our entire park has step free access, we also have wheelchairs available to borrow on request and if you require further assistance traveling around the park we have dedicated team members who can assist you.

: Locker Room

We have lockers available at our park and each locker is free to rent! To access the lockers you will need to scan your park ticket.

: Food Canteen Hall

We have over 10 unique food services on site at our canteen hall so we are able to cater for everyone! We have a wide variety including; Hot dogs stand, Mexican street food, Traditional Chinese food, Pizza & Pasta restaurant, Fast Food restaurant and our Finest Vegan cuisine!

: Shopping District

You can find all our shops within the shopping district, from gift shops, to designer boutiques our theme park has it all! If you do require assistance regarding locating a specific item you wish to purchase, please contact a member of staff.
