Marvel Universe Theme Park -
Attractions

Hover over an icon above to view the ride's name! If you would like more details on the ride, click on a red icon.
'View All' button must be selected to use the interactive park map.

Into The Spiderverse

Travel through different dimensions with Spiderman on this adventurous roller coaster ride! Through a series of twists, turns and loops, you will get to experience the thrill of swinging through the city.

The Superhero Slide

Join the world's mightiest superheroes in a water adventure unlike anything you've ever seen. The Superhero Splash shoots you down a standing trap door into a high-speed fall, passing through colorful scenes and sound effects that let you experience the thrill of being a superhero.

Hulk Smash

Unleash your inner Hulk and smash your way through this thrilling roller coaster that tests your courage and adrenaline. With speeds reaching 125 kilometers per hour, this ride will have you clinging to the edge of your seat!

Dr.Strange's Mystical Realm

Step into the magical realm and discover the secrets of the multiverse with Doctor Strange in this virtual reality ride. You will get to experience mind-bending illusions and 4D effects such as water and wind that will leave you mesmerized!

Guardians of the Galaxy Cosmos

Blast off into the cosmos on this Guardians of the Galaxy ferris wheel. Standing 180 feet in the air, this ride lets you enjoy the breathtaking view of the park and beyond.

The Avengers Tower

Visit the headquarters of Stark Industries and the Avengers as you explore The Avengers Tower. From cutting-edge technology gadgets to a thrilling Iron Man ride, you will feel like you have stepped into the Marvel universe.

Wakanda Waters

Explore the wonders of Black Panther's kingdom as you step into this futuristic boat ride! From waterfalls, mountains, tribal music, and stone statues, this boat ride will be one you'll never forget.

Avengers Splash Zone

Team up with the Avengers in this spinning water ride that uses water cannons, sprinklers and jets to give you a wet and wild experience. This is a ride you don't want to miss!

Ant-Man Microverse

Embark on an adventure with Ant-Man in this virtual reality ride, where you will shrink down to the size of ant and explore the Microverse, fight villains and overcome challenges along the way!

Thor's Hammer Swing

Possess the power of Thor as you swing through the air at a height of up to 100 meters. Don't miss the incredible view of the park!

Wolverine's X-Treme Adventure

Wolverine's X-Treme Adventure will take you on the ride of your lifetime! Accompanied with sounds and special effects, you will progress your way through breathtaking scenes of the wilderness, powerful rapids and three high velocity drops. In the final moments of the ride, you will enter Wolverine's cave, where a waterfall drop awaits your exit!

Marvel Universe Restaurant

Whether you're looking for a light snack or a three-course meal, this restaurant is the perfect place for Marvel fans who want to enjoy a delicious meal. From Spiderman Spaghetti to Captain America pizza, we've got it all!

Avengers Assemble Theatre

Come down to the theatre to see your favourite superheroes come to life! Watch the Avengers save the world or take pictures with Spiderman at the Avengers Assemble Theatre.

Marvel Universe Gift Shop

Make your trip unforgettable by finding a souvenir or a gift, for yourself or your friends, so that you can cherish your adventure for years to come. Whether you're looking for a shirt,a mug or a collectible figure, you are sure to find something that suits your taste.
