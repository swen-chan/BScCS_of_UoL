Alice's Adventures in Wonderland by Lewis Carroll

Alice's Adventures in Wonderland is an 1865 novel written by English author Lewis Carroll which tells of a girl named Alice falling through a rabbit hole into a fantasy world populated by peculiar, anthropomorphic creatures.
